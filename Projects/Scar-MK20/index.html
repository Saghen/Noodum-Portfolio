SCAR Mk 20

Sure are a lot of SCAR's being posted around lately. Why not post another?

Don't have much to say about this other than the fact that I've been working on this for quite a while, huge thanks to Stefan Engdahl for providing me with texturing and presentation advice!

Modeled in 3ds Max/ZBrush
Unwrapped in UVLayout
Baked and presented in Marmoset Toolbag 3
Textured in Substance Painter

24110 tris, 5 materials:
1x 4096x2048
3x 2048x2048
1x 2048x1024
