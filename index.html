About/Contact Me

I am a freelance 3d artist with more than 4 years of modeling and texturing experience. I specialize in game art, and am experienced in every aspect of game art production - from creating a model to putting it into the engine. Every single one of my pieces is crafted with utmost attention to detail, and produced to match standards of various triple A titles out there, while also keeping it relatively performance friendly.

Time is money these days, so I am able to fully respect deadlines and also deliver content before they are due.

My personal work experience involves working with several indie titles, as well as commission work for other clients.
